About Me

Hi there! My name is Drew Atienza and I am currently living in Selma, CA. I'm passionate about 3 things. First, I'm passionate about my family. They are the world to me. Second, I'm passionate about Liverpool Football Club. For those who are not aware of Liverpool FC, they the greatest soccer club in history (that is not up for debate). And third, I'm passionate about technology. I love learning new technologies and building new things through code.

For the past couple of years I have been studying programming and web development on my own. I've taken courses on Udemy, Treehouse, Lynda, CodeAcademy and other places. I am looking for a new challenge that I can start right now, so I am currently open to new opportunities to join a team where I can contribute while also continuing to learn and develop as a programmer.

I learn fast, work hard, and I am a team player who is proficient in an array of programming languages and tools.
